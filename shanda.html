Salami and Cream Cheese Sandwich


Satisfy your inner glutton with this savory and filling sandwich. All you need are five ingredients to make the best sandwich ever!

Servings: 1
Prep Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon grainy mustard
  • 2 slices rye bread
  • 6 slices salami, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 of a small bunch of argula, trimmed and washed
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese

Directions

  1. Spread mustard on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread cream cheese on the other slice of bread.
  3. Top with salami and arugula. Close sandwich, cut in half, and enjoy!
